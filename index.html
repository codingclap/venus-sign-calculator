Venus Sign Calculator

Named after the goddess of beauty and love, the planet Venus plays a major role in your relationships. The house it resides in on your birth chart reveals what you look for and how you express yourself in relationships, both casual and romantic. Its position can illuminate your stellar social attributes as well as your potential pitfalls, giving you valuable insights that will help strengthen and improve partnerships throughout your life.

Talents

Weakness
